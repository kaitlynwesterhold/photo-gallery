Town Of Blacksburg Old Town Hall

This is a picture of the Old Town Hall building in downtown Blacksburg. This building was established in 1798.

Carol Lee Donuts

Carol Lee Donuts has the best donuts in Blacksburg. If you are ever craving donuts Carol Lee is the place to go!

Benny's Pizza

Benny Marzano's is my favorite place to get pizza in Blacksburg. Benny's is known for its huge slices. In the picture you can tell the slice is bigger than my friend's head!

Blacksburg Farmers Market Flag

This flag is hung up by the Blacksburg Farmers Market. The Blacksburg Farmer Market is open on Saturdays from 12-6 p.m. and Wednesday's from 8 a.m to 2 p.m.

A Booth at the Blacksburg Farmers Market

This is one of the booths at the Blacksburg Farmers Market. They are selling pumpkins, flowers, and locally grown fruits and vegetable.

Blacksburg Market Square Park

This is the sign for the Blacksburg Square Market. The Blacksburg Square Market is where the Farmers Market is held.

A Mural in Downtown Blacksburg

This mural is located to the left of the Farmers Market. It uses a variety of colors and images to describe pieces of Blacksburg such as; the Huckleberry Trail, Market Square Park, and the Alexander Black House.

Another Mural in Downtown Blacksburg

Walking around in downtown Blacksburg you see murals on a lot of the buildings. This is a mural of a boom box in one of the back alley's of downtown. I love the creativity in all the murals downtown.

Main Street

Main street is the heart of Blacksburg. You can find everything on Main Street from dinner, to shopping, to all the Virginia Tech gear you could need.

A Virginia Tech BT Bus

This is one of the many BT buses. The coolest part about the buses are the different images on the side of each bus. This bus demonstrates the Virginia Tech motto "No Hokie Left Behind".
